About the College

Late Ambubai Women's Arts, Science, and Commerce College, Nillod, Taluka Sillod, District Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, was established in the academic year 2022–23 by Samarth Pratishthan, Gavhali. Founded under the visionary leadership of Hon. Shri Sachin Ingle (President) and Hon. Smt. Vandana Kulkarni (Ingle) (Secretary), the college is affiliated with S.N.D.T. Women's University, Mumbai. The institution was established with the mission of providing quality higher education to women, particularly those from rural and economically disadvantaged backgrounds, empowering them through knowledge, skills, and values.

The college currently offers B.A., B.Sc. (CLS), M.Sc. (Chemistry), and B.A. in Fashion Design programmes, supported by modern teaching-learning facilities, laboratories, a library, and skill development initiatives. Committed to academic excellence and holistic development, the institution strives to create confident, self-reliant, and socially responsible women while expanding educational opportunities for girls in rural communities.

Vision

"Empowering Women through Knowledge, Values, and Excellence."

"To be a centre of excellence in women's higher education by empowering young women through quality, inclusive, value-based, and transformative education, enabling them to become confident, self-reliant, socially responsible, and globally competent citizens."

Mission

"Empowering Women, Enriching Society."

  • Provide quality and inclusive higher education for women.
  • Promote academic excellence, research, innovation, and skill development.
  • Develop ethical, confident, and socially responsible women leaders.
  • Enhance employability through industry collaboration and experiential learning.
  • Ensure quality, equality, and holistic development in a safe learning environment.
